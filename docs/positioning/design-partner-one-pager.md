# Decision Intel — Design Partner Program

**Five seats. Twelve months. Fortune 500 corporate strategy teams.**

---

## What the program is

A focused 12-month engagement with five Fortune 500 corporate strategy teams who want to shape **the native system of record for strategic reasoning** as it is being built. Design partners pay **$1,999/month** — a 20% concession off the $2,499 Strategy-tier list price — and in return they get the most direct line into the product, the founder, and the Recognition-Rigor Framework (R²F) vocabulary as it becomes an owned category.

We charge because each audit runs seventeen LLM calls across twelve specialized nodes and carries real infrastructure cost. Free pilots produce lead attitude, not customer attitude. The concession is the relationship signal; the fee is the commitment.

---

## What a design partner receives

1. **$1,999/month locked for 12 months.** 20% off the $2,499 Strategy list. First right of refusal at $2,499 for Year 2 before list moves again.
2. **Decision Provenance Record on every audit — free.** The signed, hashed 4-page PDF your General Counsel hands to the audit committee: input-document hash, prompt fingerprint, model lineage, academic citations across the 30+ bias taxonomy, regulatory mapping across seven frameworks, pipeline lineage. Normally bundled into Strategy+ in Year 2; free for the design-partner cohort.
3. **Custom toxic-combination weights tuned to your industry.** The founder personally calibrates the 20×20 interaction matrix using your historical memo library.
4. **Direct Slack channel with the founder.** Bug reports and feature requests route to the same Slack the founder writes code in. No ticket queues.
5. **Quarterly 1-hour strategy session.** Memo rehearsal, board-deck review, or a direct run through the R²F framework.
6. **Early access to new capabilities.** R²F Playbook, Weekly Digest, Decision Rooms, outcome-learning recalibration — design-partner Slack is invited before the public changelog.
7. **Marquee logo placement on /proof (optional, with approval).**
8. **Named design partner in investor deck (optional, with approval).**

4. **First month's $1,999 paid up-front as the commitment fee.** Lands before kickoff. The commitment IS the fee — free pilots produce lead attitude, not customer attitude.
5. **Signed Master Service Agreement + Data Processing Addendum.** MSA template in `docs/positioning/design-partner-msa-template.md`.

## Who we're looking for

**Primary (4 of 5 seats):** Fortune 500 corporate strategy function at banking, insurance, pharma, aerospace, or energy. Teams of 3–15. Producing formal strategic memos or board papers quarterly. Regulatory vocabulary (Basel III, EU AI Act, FDA, FAA, SEC Reg D) is native to your buyer conversations.

**Secondary (1 of 5 seats):** M&A-heavy operator — conglomerate, PE portco with formal M&A discipline, or corporate development function at a Fortune 500. Different use case (deal memos, not strategic memos) but validates the dual-stream positioning.

**Deliberately not targeting:** tech, consumer, DTC, early-stage. They move too fast for structured decision-quality discipline, pay less, and the logo carries less procurement weight with future enterprise buyers.
