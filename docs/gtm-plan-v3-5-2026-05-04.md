## §3 Risks + empirical mitigations (locked)

Top 5 risks across the 6-year sequence:

1. **Continuity Chasm (#1 risk).** Phase 1 dilutes the wedge persona with non-decision-makers (e.g., junior analysts) → MQL-to-SQL collapses to the 15-21% industry baseline → no graduation, no Phase 2 fuel. **Mitigation:** strict ICP gating in marketing copy + onboarding flow. Reject sign-ups outside the four buyer-class-continuous personas at the application form, with a polite "we're not yet built for your role; here's a different recommendation" auto-response.

2. **Founder burnout (solo-founder constraint).** Solo technical founder + Phase 1 customer-facing motion + product roadmap + Vohra survey discipline = unsustainable at high cadence. **Mitigation:** relentless onboarding automation. Self-serve sign-up + automated DPR generation + automated Vohra survey + automated outcome-gate prompts + automated case-study capture (per v3.5 metrics dashboard discipline below). Founder-led sales only on the top 5 highest-conviction prospects.

3. **Premature Enterprise Escalation (single most likely failure mode).** Founder activates Mr. Reiner / Mr. Gabe networks before Phase 1 produces paid customers + Phase 2 produces the Sankore reference. Drags solo founder into perpetual custom integrations, stalls product development, exhausts $200K runway. **Mitigation:** lock Phase 3 activation criteria (5 paid + 3 ROI case studies + Sankore reference) as non-negotiable. Refuse custom enterprise PoCs until Phase 3 is fully operational.

4. **Schema drift in team expansion.** Notion-style flexibility produces duplicated truth + permission chaos at team scale → enterprise procurement breaks. **Mitigation:** opinionated rigid Linear-style workflows for the R²F pipeline + DPR generation + DQI scoring. Resist "flexibility" feature requests. The architecture decision IS the moat.

5. **Runway depletion.** $200K + Phase 1 motion = ~10-12 months of runway. If Phase 1 baseline doesn't fire, runway crosses zero before kill criterion can fire. **Mitigation:** secure annual prepay discount from day one (negative working capital), keep monthly burn <£20K, defer all non-essential spend (legal entity formation, SOC 2 Type II audit, paid acquisition) until Phase 2 or later.
