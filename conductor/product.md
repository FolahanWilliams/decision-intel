# Product Definition: Decision Intelligence Platform

## Mission
To build the world's most robust "Decision Intelligence" platform that audits business documents for cognitive bias, fact-checking, and decision noise.

## Core Value Proposition
*   **Bias Detection**: Identifies 15 specific neurocognitive distortions (e.g., Sunk Cost, Groupthink).
*   **Noise Audit**: Uses multi-agent variance (3-Judge System) to measure decision instability.
*   **Fact Checking**: Real-time verification of financial claims using FMP (Financial Modeling Prep).
*   **Privacy**: Fully GDPR-compliant redaction pipeline.

## Target Audience
*   Investment Committees
*   Board of Directors
*   Compliance Officers
*   Legal Audit Teams
