# Product Definition: Decision Intelligence Platform

## Mission
To build the definitive cognitive bias audit engine for PE/VC investment committees — detecting the biases that destroy fund returns before capital is deployed.

## Core Value Proposition
*   **PE/VC Bias Detection**: Identifies 11 investment-specific biases (anchoring to entry valuation, carry incentive distortion, winner's curse, etc.) plus 20 general cognitive biases.
*   **Deal Pipeline Intelligence**: Full deal lifecycle tracking from screening through exit, with stage-specific bias overlays for IC memos, CIMs, pitch decks, and term sheets.
*   **Noise Audit**: Uses multi-agent variance (3-Judge System) to measure IC decision inconsistency across valuation, timing, framing, and comparable selection dimensions.
*   **Outcome Flywheel**: Links deal outcomes (IRR, MOIC) to detected biases — building institutional memory of what biases actually hurt returns at your firm.
*   **IC Simulation**: Simulates boardroom deliberations with PE-specific personas (Managing Partner, Operating Partner, LP Rep, Sector Expert, Risk Committee Chair).
*   **Privacy**: Fully GDPR-compliant redaction pipeline — PII stripped before any AI analysis.

## Beachhead Market
*   **PE/VC Investment Committees** (~7,500 firms globally)
*   Deal partners, analysts, and IC members making $50M-$500M capital allocation decisions

## Future Market Expansion
*   Asset management firms
*   Corporate development / M&A teams
*   Board of Directors
*   Compliance Officers
